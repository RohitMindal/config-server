Our Medical Services

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care with advanced cardiac procedures, heart disease prevention, and rehabilitation services.

  • Cardiac catheterization
  • Heart surgery
  • Pacemaker implantation
  • Heart failure management

Neurology

Expert neurological care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system with cutting-edge treatments.

  • Stroke treatment
  • Epilepsy management
  • Brain tumor surgery
  • Spinal cord treatments

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment.

  • Newborn care
  • Childhood vaccinations
  • Pediatric surgery
  • Growth & development

Orthopedics

Advanced bone, joint, and muscle care with minimally invasive procedures and rehabilitation.

  • Joint replacement
  • Sports medicine
  • Fracture treatment
  • Arthritis management

Pulmonology

Respiratory care for lung diseases, breathing disorders, and critical pulmonary conditions.

  • Asthma treatment
  • COPD management
  • Sleep disorders
  • Lung cancer care

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive women's health services from pregnancy care to gynecological treatments.

  • Prenatal care
  • Labor & delivery
  • Gynecological surgery
  • Family planning
